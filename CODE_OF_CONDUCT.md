# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

VIDMARK STUDIO should be a practical, respectful project for creators and developers.

Contributors are expected to:

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing private media or personal information.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, personal attacks, doxxing, publishing private files, or intentionally disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.
